WE ARE OLYMPUS ROBOTICS

Our Team

Olympus Robotics is a dedicated group of students and mentors driven by a passion for engineering and technology. Based in Fort Wayne, Indiana, we are a FRC team that is committed to using our skills and knowledge to make a positive impact in our community and beyond. We have had the opportunity to learn and grow through FRC, using the principles of engineering to build and program robots that are capable of tackling complex challenges. But our involvement in the FRC goes beyond just building robots - we are also dedicated to giving back to the community through outreach and service projects.
